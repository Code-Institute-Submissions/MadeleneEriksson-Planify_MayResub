Why stay organized?

Mental health

Decreasing stress and anxiety. By decluttering your living spaces, you can decrease housework by 40%. Staying organized will actually lower your cortisol-levels - which is the body's primary stress hormone.

Better Sleep

By prioritizing how your day is best spent, you give yourself time to focus on what really matters. At nighttime you are able to prioritize sleep, rest knowing that all you had to do today is done.

Productivity

One of the biggest enemys of creativity is stress. By decluttering and organizing your workspace you can focus on work and limit your distractions.

Healtier diet

Being organizied allows you to plan healty dishes and meal prep for the week. When you've prepared your meals and snacks ahead of time, you're less likely to make poor dietary choices.
